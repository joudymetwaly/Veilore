Our Textile Engineering USP

Why Veilore completely eliminates the frustrating need for underlying layers.

1. Heavy Crepe / Crepe Marocain

Why is it perfect: Standard crepe can be thin, but Heavy Crepe or Crepe Marocain has a beautiful weight to it. The signature pebble-textured surface naturally diffuses light, eliminating the see-through effect completely. It drapes gracefully, skips over the body instead of sticking, and does not wrinkle easily.

2. Cotton Poplin

Why is it perfect: This is a tightly woven, high-thread-count cotton weave. Unlike cheap cotton sheets, high-quality poplin is dense and completely opaque while remaining entirely breathable. It gives blouses that crisp, sharp, "expensive" collar and cuff look.

3. Premium Velour / Velvet

Why is it perfect: The plush, thick pile of quality velour blocks all light. It provides maximum coverage and warmth, making it ideal for elegant, premium winter skirts and blouses.

4. Ponte Knit

Why is it perfect: It is a thick, premium double-knit fabric. Because it is knitted twice, it has a built-in density that makes it 100% opaque and completely zero see-through. It holds its shape perfectly, smooths out lines, and gives a very professional, high-end look without clinging to the body.
